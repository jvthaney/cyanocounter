    • Customize counters for plankton taxa



    • 1. Enter your information above and click "Submit Info" to log it.
      • Default values follow LSPA protocols (2 m depth, LSPA net ring, 20 rows, 1 ml Sedgewick Rafter volume).
      • Update any value and click Submit Info again to recalculate.
    • 2. Count colonies or filaments (not individual cells). Click "Add counter +" for each new taxon, enter its name, then use + / − to tally as you traverse the slide.
      • If a count threshold is set, a progress bar will track your total. Stop counting when it turns orange (threshold reached) or when you complete the slide, whichever comes first.
      • You can preview data at the bottom of the page at any time.
    • 3. When done, click "Export data" to download a .CSV file.
